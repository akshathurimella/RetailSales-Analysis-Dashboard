# 🛍️ Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
The **Retail Sales Performance Dashboard** is designed to provide actionable insights into retail operations. It consolidates sales, customer behavior, and product performance into a single interactive view, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **Executive KPIs**: Product count, total orders, gross income, average quantity, average rating, and total sales.  
- **Payment Analysis**: Distribution of sales across cash, credit card, and e-wallet.  
- **Product Line Performance**: Orders, gross income, and total sales by category.  
- **Customer Demographics**: Gender-based sales comparison and customer type breakdown.  
- **Monthly Sales Trends**: Line graph showing seasonal fluctuations in sales.  
- **Product Ratings**: Average ratings by product category.  
- **Interactive Filters**: Branch, gender, payment type, customer type, and product selection.  

---
